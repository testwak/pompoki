Installation #

To install the @pompoki bot in your guild or any other guild, follow these steps:

  1. Invite the Bot: Click here to invite the bot to your access guild.
  2. Select Server: Choose the server where you want to add @pompoki. You must have the Manage Server permission in that server.
  3. Grant Permissions: Ensure the bot has the necessary permissions to function correctly. While the exact permissions required for @pompoki are not specified in the provided sources, it's common for @pompoki to need permissions like:
    • Send Messages
    • Embed Links
    • Message History
    • Manage Messages (delete messages)
    • Use External Emojis (custom emojis)
    These permissions allow the bot to interact with users and perform its functions effectively.
  4. Authorize the Bot: After selecting the permissions, click Authorize to add the bot to your server.

    5. Note: @pompoki may require additional permissions depending on its features. Always review the permissions requested and ensure they align with the bot's intended functionality.
